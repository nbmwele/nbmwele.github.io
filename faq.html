Frequently Asked Questions

Could you please send me the catalog & price list?

You can download the product catalog on the Pages/Product page.

If you see a product you are interested in in the catalog, we will make a price list for you accordingly.

Can you provide free samples for testing?

Of course, we can provide free samples.

We understand the concerns of new customers very well, and we are very confident in our products, both in terms of price and quality.

Can you put our own label on the product?

No problem, most of our products support customers' trademarks.

To be honest, we hope to jointly promote the MINGWAY brand.

How to become a distributor in my country?

Generally, we hope that dealers can meet the following qualifications:
1. There are some local sales networks;
2. Good technical solution skills;
3. Keep inventory to meet fast delivery time;
4. Have a dedicated team to sell and promote.

You are very welcome to tell us your thoughts on the above matters; We also very much hope to create value for you through our products.
